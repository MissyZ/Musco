SportsCluster Green™

SportsCluster Green™ provides reliable, cost-efficient lighting for arenas, field houses, and other large indoor areas.

  • Saves up to 25% in energy and life-cycle costs compared to
    previous technologies
  • Provides precise control of lighting, minimizing shadows, and
    scoreboard washout
  • Designed as a modular system – easily adapting to catwalks, trusses, and other interior structures – with remote ballast enclosures for
    easy maintenance
  • Meets players, spectator, and broadcast lighting requirements
  • Factory assembled, aimed and tested for proven performance
