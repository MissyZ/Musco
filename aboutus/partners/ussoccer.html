U.S. Soccer Foundation

Musco is proud to be a Corporate Partner of the U.S. Soccer Foundation®, the major charitable arm of soccer in the United States. When it comes to sports field lighting, you'll find Musco makes it easy. Our problem-solving capabilities will address every aspect of your lighting project. From funding to volunteer installation, Musco can help you make it happen, meeting lighting standards approved by the U.S. Soccer Foundation with Light-Structure Green™ — a foundation to poletop system.

Musco will design a lighting system that's exactly right for your field, making your project one you can be proud of for many years. Our experience with thousands of successful projects assures great results, and saves you time and money — today and tomorrow.
