European Hockey Federation

Musco Lighting is proud to be the approved Sports Lighting System of the European Hockey Federation. When it comes to pitch lighting, you'll find that Musco makes it easy. Our problem-solving capabilities will address every aspect of your lighting project. Musco can help you make it happen, meeting EHF criteria with Light-Structure Green™ - the approved Sports-Lighting System of the European Hockey Federation.

Musco will design a lighting system that's exactly right for your pitch, making your project one you can be proud of for many years. Our experience with thousands of successful projects assures great results, and saves you time and money - today and tomorrow.
