Amateur Softball Association

Since 1999, Musco Lighting has been a proud partner of the Amateur Softball Association. When it comes to ballfield lighting, you'll find that Musco makes it easy. Our problem-solving capabilities will address every aspect of your lighting project. From funding to volunteer installation, Musco can help you make it happen, meeting ASA guidelines with Light-Structure Green™ lighting system, which is the Official Sports-Lighting System of the Amateur Softball Association.

Musco will design a lighting system that's exactly right for your field, making your project one you can be proud of for many years. Our experience with thousands of successful projects assures great results, and saves you time and money — today and tomorrow.
