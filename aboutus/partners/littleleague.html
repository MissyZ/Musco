Little League® Baseball and Softball

Since 1992, Musco Lighting has been a proud partner of Little League® Baseball and Softball. When it comes to ballfield lighting, you’ll find that Musco makes it easy. Our problem-solving capabilities will address every aspect of your lighting project. From funding to volunteer installation, Musco can help you make it happen, meeting Little League standards with Light-Structure Green™ — the Official Sports-Lighting System of Little League® Baseball and Softball.

Musco will design a lighting system that's exactly right for your field, making your project one you can be proud of for many years. Our experience with thousands of successful projects assures great results, and saves you time and money — today and tomorrow.
