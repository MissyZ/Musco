Sports Lighting Solutions

From youth sports fields to professional stadiums, Musco is the industry leader in sports lighting. We bring more than 35 years of experience, designing lighting systems to meet the needs of our customers.

Musco manufactures innovative sports lighting solutions using LED and metal halide light sources to significantly lower energy consumption, while reducing the environmental impact of spill light and glare. Musco's proven system design incorporates lighting, structural, and electrical components to provide guaranteed performance and trouble-free installation.
