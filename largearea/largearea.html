Large Area Lighting Solutions

Musco offers lighting solutions for a wide range of lighting applications. Our engineers will work with you to create energy- efficient solutions designed to meet your specific needs. Our customized lighting systems will provide the best quality of light, while minimizing off-site spill light and glare and reducing your facility’s energy usage.

We can create complete lighting systems for:

• Airports • Parking Lots • Distribution Centers
• Construction Sites • Industrial Facilities • Ports
• Bridges • Rail Yards • Facade
