Air + Style Show

Pasadena, California, USA

Air + Style becomes one of the first sporting events to use a mobile LED System

For more than 20 years, the popular Air + Style Show has remained a tradition for freestyle skiers, snowboarders, and music aficionados. Air + Style is a two-day action sports competition and music concert. But when the show decided to conclude its final outing of the year by staging its first event in the United States, the Air + Style Show needed a trusted lighting manufacturer that could provide superior broadcast lighting with special effects.

That's where Musco stepped in. As Musco Mobile's first project using an LED light source, the Musco Team quickly engineered and designed a system to meet the quick timetable, while meeting the lighting necessities for the unique events and broadcast requirements. Musco provided lighting for the Big Air ramp, which was nearly 16 stories tall, helping the storied event held at the Rose Bowl in Pasadena, California, continue its successful tradition.

 

“In 1982, we developed a state-of-the-art mobile lighting system for the first televised night college football game held at Notre Dame Stadium. Today, we're continuing that tradition of innovation by incorporating the LED light source in our mobile systems for an even more enhanced result.”

— Jeff Rogers
Vice President of Developmental Sales
