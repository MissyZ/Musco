AT&T Center Chooses Musco’s LED Lighting Solution

As part of a major infrastructure and technology renovation at the San Antonio Spurs’ AT&T Center, Bexar County and Spurs Sports & Entertainment partnered with Musco to install a new state-of-the-art LED lighting system that maximized energy efficiency and revamped the entertainment experience at the venue.

Musco’s customized lighting solution includes advanced optics that provide the venue with unprecedented light control and precision. By providing a brighter and more uniform light distribution onto the playing surface, the system dramatically reduces glare, improves visibility for athletes, and creates a stage-like atmosphere for spectators.

The lighting solution offers superior broadcast quality lighting without the “flicker” effect, and instant on/off capabilities allow flexibility for special effects, and eliminate warm-up time for the lamps to reach full light levels.

The AT&T Center will see a significant reduction in operating costs with a 78 percent reduction in energy consumption compared to the prior lights and Musco’s 10-year parts and labor warranty. Backed by Musco’s team of technicians, this comprehensive warranty eliminates maintenance costs for the next decade.

   

“Technology enhancements are an important focal point of this renovation project. The new lighting system is a visible improvement that will help enhance the fan experience while reducing energy consumption and maintenance costs.”

— Lori Warren
SVP of Corporate Finance and Strategy
Spurs Sports and Entertainment
