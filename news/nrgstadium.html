NRG Stadium

Houston, Texas

Continuing a Legacy of Innovation at NRG Stadium
The home of the Houston Texans installs Musco's LED lighting solution

NRG Stadium is home to the Houston Texans and the Houston Livestock Show and Rodeoâ„¢ and has
been a beacon of innovation for stadiums across the nation. It was the first Rodeo and professional football indoor/outdoor retractable roof, natural grass stadium. It will be the first professional stadium in Texas to install solar panels to power the stadium. And, it's now one of the first professional football stadiums in the country to feature new lighting solution using an LED light source.

Without question, NRG Stadium is on the leading edge of professional sports stadiums when it comes to sustainable practices. The 700 solar panels, along with six electrical vehicle-charging stations, are other aspects of the venue's sustainability master plan. The new, energy efficient LED lighting system was one of the major components of this comprehensive plan.

To make the plan a reality, the leaders at NRG turned to a partner they could trust. Musco installed
the stadium's original lights when it was built in 2002, and is the world's leading innovator in sports
lighting solutions.

The field lighting involved the installation of 480 optically enhanced, high efficiency LED fixtures, which ultimately produce a much brighter, more uniformly distributed light throughout the stadium. In addition to an enhanced playing and fan experience, the system offers instant on/off/dimming capabilities and the ability for special effects during Texans' games or at any of the other major sporting and non-sporting events held at NRG Stadium throughout the year. The lighting system will help the stadium realize a significant savings in operating costs, with the virtual elimination of maintenance and increased energy conservation. And the lights will function at a much higher level of efficiency, with a total energy savings of 60%.
