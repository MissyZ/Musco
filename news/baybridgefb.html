Bay Bridge East Span

San Francisco â€¢ Oakland, CA

For more than three years Musco has been working with the collaborative design team for the new East Span of the San Francisco/Oakland Bay Bridge.

The bridge opened to traffic on September 3, 2013, with a portion of Musco's custom-designed LED lighting system installed. The balance of the lighting system is scheduled to be completed over the next several months, as the final bridge construction is completed. Our Team is proud to be involved with this iconic project.
